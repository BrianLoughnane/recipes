Ingredients:

8 tsp Vegetable Better Than Bouillon
8 cups water
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
5 cloves garlic
1 head bok choy
20 baby carrots
1/2 large sweet potato
1 tbsp ghee
1/2 cup red lentils

Directions:

Add all ingredients into pressure cooker
Add salt, pepper, turmeric, onion powder
Cook low for 20 minutes
